# Process Compressor Maintenance

## Purpose

Routine maintenance procedures for reciprocating and screw-type process compressors used in oil refinery operations.

---

## Safety Warnings

- Apply Lock Out / Tag Out (LOTO) before performing any maintenance.
- Vent residual process pressure before opening any connection.
- Compressor surfaces may remain hot after operation. Allow sufficient cooling time.
- Ensure adequate ventilation within compressor enclosures.

---

## Daily Checks

1. Check the oil level and condition.
2. Inspect for hydrocarbon or oil leaks around connections.
3. Verify that operating pressures are within the normal range.
4. Check vibration levels. If abnormal vibration is detected, stop the compressor and investigate.
5. Confirm correct operation of the cooling system (fan, coolant level).
6. Review the control panel for active alarms.

---

## Weekly Maintenance

1. Drain condensate from receivers and separators.
2. Inspect and clean the air intake filters.
3. Inspect drive belts for wear and proper tension.
4. Verify the operation of all safety valves (visual inspection only – do NOT manually lift).
5. Record operating hours.

---

## Monthly Maintenance

1. Perform oil sampling and analysis.
2. Check valve clearances (reciprocating compressors).
3. Inspect gland packing for leakage.
4. Test automatic shutdown systems.
5. Clean or replace oil filters.

---

## Annual Maintenance

1. Perform a complete overhaul of suction and discharge valves.
2. Replace piston rings and rider rings (reciprocating compressors).
3. Inspect rotor condition (screw compressors).
4. Pressure-test all safety valves.
5. Perform a complete alignment check.
6. Review and update the preventive maintenance plan.

---

## Common Fault Codes

| Code | Description | Recommended Action |
|------|-------------|--------------------|
| C-01 | High discharge temperature | Check cooling system and clean the cooler |
| C-02 | Low oil pressure | Check oil level and inspect the oil pump |
| C-03 | High vibration | Check alignment and inspect bearings |
| C-04 | Overload trip | Verify process conditions and inspect valves |
| C-05 | Seal gas pressure low | Inspect seal gas system and replace filters if required |
