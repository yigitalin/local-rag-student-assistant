# PPE Requirements for Oil Refinery Operations

## Purpose

Mandatory Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) requirements for all oil refinery operations.

---

## Safety Warning

- Entry to ANY refinery process area without the required PPE is PROHIBITED.
- PPE must be inspected before each use. Do NOT use damaged equipment.

---

## Standard PPE (All Site Activities)

| Item | Standard | Inspection Frequency |
|------|----------|----------------------|
| Hard hat | EN 397 | Before each use |
| Safety glasses | EN 166 | Before each use |
| Flame-resistant coveralls | EN 11612 | Before each use |
| Steel-toe safety boots | EN ISO 20345 S3 | Before each use |
| Cut-resistant gloves | EN 388 Level 3+ | Before each use |
| High-visibility vest | EN ISO 20471 Class 2 | Before each use |

---

## Additional PPE (Hazardous Area Work)

| Item | Standard | When Required |
|------|----------|---------------|
| Portable hydrocarbon gas detector | EN 60079-29-1 | Hazardous process areas |
| SCBA (Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus) | EN 137 | Confined space / High LEL |
| Chemical-resistant gloves | EN 374 | Chemical handling |
| Face shield | EN 166 B | Pressure testing |
| Hearing protection | EN 352 | Noise >85 dB(A) |
| Fall arrest harness | EN 361 | Working at height >2 m |

---

## Hydrocarbon Gas Detector Requirements

- Must be bump-tested before each shift.
- Full calibration at least every 30 days.
- Alarm settings:
  - Low: 10% LEL
  - High: 20% LEL
  - TWA: 1 ppm H₂S
  - STEL: 5 ppm H₂S

---

## Inspection and Replacement

- Inspect all PPE before each use.
- Replace hard hats every 3 years or immediately after any impact.
- Replace fall arrest harnesses every 5 years or after any arrest event.
- Replace flame-resistant clothing if its protective properties are compromised.
