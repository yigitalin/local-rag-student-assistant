# Pipeline Integrity Inspection

## Purpose
Procedures for assessing the structural integrity of gas transmission and distribution pipelines.

## Safety Warning
- Pipeline inspection may require working near live gas – continuous gas monitoring mandatory.
- Do NOT excavate near pipelines without confirmed location (CAT scan + signal generator).
- Maintain minimum clearance from live services.

## Inspection Types

### 1. Visual Inspection (External)
- Coating condition (disbondment, cracking, holidays)
- Corrosion (surface rust, pitting, wall loss)
- Mechanical damage (dents, gouges, scrapes)
- Ground movement indicators (soil cracking, subsidence)
- Third-party activity near pipeline corridor

### 2. Coating Survey
- DCVG (Direct Current Voltage Gradient) survey for coating defects
- CIPS (Close Interval Potential Survey) for cathodic protection levels

### 3. Wall Thickness Measurement
- Ultrasonic thickness testing (UT) at predetermined points
- Record readings and compare with previous surveys
- **Minimum wall thickness:** per API 5L or design specification
- Flag any reading below 87.5% of nominal wall thickness

### 4. Cathodic Protection Check
- Measure pipe-to-soil potential at test posts
- Acceptable range: -850 mV to -1200 mV (Cu/CuSO₄ reference)
- Record rectifier output (voltage and current)
- Check anode bed condition if potential is inadequate

## Procedure
1. Obtain work permit and confirm pipeline location.
2. Set up gas monitoring in work area.
3. If excavation required, hand-dig within 500mm of pipeline.
4. Clean pipe surface at inspection points.
5. Perform visual inspection and photograph findings.
6. Conduct UT wall thickness measurements.
7. Measure pipe-to-soil potential.
8. Record all data on Pipeline Inspection Report (PIR-001).
9. Backfill and reinstate to standard.

## Defect Classification
| Category | Description | Response Time |
|----------|-------------|---------------|
| Priority 1 | Active leak or wall loss >40% | Immediate action |
| Priority 2 | Significant coating defect, CP low | Within 7 days |
| Priority 3 | Minor coating damage, cosmetic | Next planned maintenance |
