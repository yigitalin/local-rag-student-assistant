# Common Fault Codes – Hydrocarbon Gas Detection Equipment

## Purpose

Quick reference for fault codes displayed by portable hydrocarbon gas detection equipment used in oil refinery operations.

---

## Safety Warning

- A faulty hydrocarbon gas detector means you have NO atmospheric protection.
- Do NOT enter hazardous areas with a malfunctioning detector.
- Replace or repair the detector immediately. Do NOT attempt field-level sensor repair.

---

## Fault Code Reference

### Code: E01 – Sensor Failure

- **Symptom:** "SENSOR FAIL" or "E01" displayed
- **Cause:** Sensor exhausted, poisoned, or disconnected
- **Action:**
  1. Power off the detector.
  2. Remove and reseat the sensor.
  3. Power on and perform a re-test.
  4. If the fault persists, replace the sensor.
  5. Send the detector for recalibration.

---

### Code: E02 – Calibration Overdue

- **Symptom:** "CAL DUE" warning
- **Cause:** Calibration interval exceeded (>30 days)
- **Action:**
  1. Do NOT use the detector for safety-critical monitoring.
  2. Return the detector to the calibration station.
  3. Perform a full span calibration.
  4. Record the new calibration date.

---

### Code: E03 – Low Battery

- **Symptom:** Battery icon flashing, "LOW BATT"
- **Cause:** Insufficient battery charge
- **Action:**
  1. Replace or recharge the battery.
  2. Do NOT continue monitoring with a low battery.
  3. If in a hazardous area, evacuate and replace the detector.

---

### Code: E04 – Over-Range

- **Symptom:** Display shows "OL" or ">100% LEL"
- **Cause:** Hydrocarbon concentration exceeds the sensor measurement range.
- **Action:**
  1. EVACUATE IMMEDIATELY.
  2. Notify the control room.
  3. Do NOT re-enter until the area has been confirmed safe.

---

### Code: E05 – Bump Test Fail

- **Symptom:** "BUMP FAIL" after bump test
- **Cause:** Sensor response outside the acceptable range
- **Action:**
  1. Repeat the bump test using certified calibration gas.
  2. If the fault persists, perform a full calibration.
  3. Use an alternative detector until the unit is repaired.

---

### Code: E06 – Pump Fault

- **Symptom:** "PUMP ERROR" (pump-equipped models only)
- **Cause:** Blocked inlet, pump failure, or diaphragm damage
- **Action:**
  1. Check the inlet for blockage.
  2. Replace the inlet filter if necessary.
  3. If the fault persists, send the detector for repair.
