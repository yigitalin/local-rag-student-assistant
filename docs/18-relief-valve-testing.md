# Relief Valve Testing and Maintenance

## Purpose
Procedures for testing and maintaining pressure relief valves (PRVs) and safety relief valves (SRVs) in gas systems.

## Safety Warning
- Relief valves are CRITICAL SAFETY DEVICES – never disable or bypass.
- Testing must be performed by trained, competent personnel.
- Use only test equipment rated for the valve's design pressure.
- Vent discharged gas safely during testing.

## Testing Frequency
| Valve Type | Test Interval | Full Overhaul |
|------------|---------------|---------------|
| PSV (spring-loaded) | 12 months | 5 years |
| Pilot-operated PRV | 12 months | 5 years |
| Thermal relief valve | 24 months | 5 years |

## In-Situ Test Procedure (Bench Test Preferred Where Possible)
1. Confirm bypass or redundant protection is available.
2. Install test gauge downstream of valve.
3. Slowly increase upstream pressure.
4. Record the pressure at which valve starts to lift (cracking pressure).
5. Record full-flow pressure (set pressure).
6. Compare with nameplate set pressure.
7. Acceptance:  ±3% of set pressure for pressures >70 bar; ±2 bar for pressures <70 bar.
8. Reduce pressure and confirm valve reseats fully.
9. Check for leakage after reseat.

## Common Faults
| Fault | Symptoms | Action |
|-------|----------|--------|
| Lift pressure too high | Valve does not open at set pressure | Spring adjustment or replacement |
| Lift pressure too low | Premature opening, gas loss | Spring adjustment or replacement |
| Leak through seat | Continuous gas discharge | Lap seat, replace seat or disc |
| Chatter | Rapid open/close cycling | Check sizing, backpressure, or piping |
| Stuck closed | No response to overpressure | Remove and bench test – likely corrosion |

## Maintenance Procedure
1. Remove valve (LOTO, depressurise, vent).
2. Disassemble and inspect all components.
3. Clean seat and disc – lap if minor damage.
4. Replace soft goods (O-rings, gaskets, diaphragms).
5. Replace spring if out of spec or corroded.
6. Reassemble and bench test to set pressure.
7. Apply test tag with date, tech ID, and next test date.
8. Reinstall and leak-test connections.
